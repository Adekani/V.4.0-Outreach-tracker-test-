Curriculum

World-Class Academics

A comprehensive, forward-looking curriculum aligned with Nigerian educational standards and global best practices — from Nursery to Senior Secondary.

School Levels

From Nursery to Senior Secondary

Early Years (Nursery)

A play-based, nurturing environment for children aged 2–5. We build foundational literacy, numeracy, and social skills through structured play and discovery.

Ages 2–5 Play-Based

Primary (Basic 1–6)

Core literacy, numeracy, science, and social studies taught by specialist teachers. Strong emphasis on reading culture, problem-solving, and critical thinking.

Ages 6–11 NERDC Aligned

Junior Secondary (JSS)

Broad-based education preparing students for specialisation. Students sit the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE) at the end of JSS 3.

Ages 11–14 BECE Prep

Senior Secondary (SSS)

Rigorous preparation for WAEC, NECO, and UTME. Students choose from Science, Commercial, or Arts tracks. Our track record of A1–B2 grades is unmatched.

WAEC / NECO JAMB Prep

Begin Your Journey

We warmly welcome applications for all levels. Admission into Excellence Academy is merit-based, and our team is here to guide you every step of the way.

How to Apply

The Admissions Process

1

Pick Up Application Form

Visit our school office or download the form online. Forms are available year-round. Obtain at the school secretariat or via the link below.

2

Submit Completed Form

Return the completed form with photocopies of last report card, birth certificate, and passport photographs. Pay the non-refundable application fee.

3

Entrance Assessment

Applicants for Primary 2 and above write a short entrance test in English, Mathematics, and General Knowledge.

4

Offer Letter & Acceptance

Successful candidates receive an offer letter within 5 working days. Acceptance is confirmed upon payment of the first-term school fees.

FAQs

Common Questions

When does the school year start?

The academic year follows the Nigerian school calendar: First Term (September–December), Second Term (January–March), Third Term (April–July). Exact resumption dates are communicated via circulars.

Do you offer bus services?

Yes. We operate school buses covering major routes across Port Harcourt. A separate transport levy is charged per term. Please contact us for current routes and availability.

Is there a feeding programme?

Nutritious hot meals are provided daily for Early Years and Nursery pupils. Optional feeding plans are available for Primary and Secondary students at an additional cost.

Are scholarships available?

We award merit-based scholarships to top-performing students at the end of each academic year. Partial fee waivers are also available for exceptional cases. Contact admissions for details.
